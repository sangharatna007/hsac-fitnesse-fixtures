    Hotel Amenities

    Hotel Amenities

    This Fifth Avenue hotel is within four short blocks of the Museum of Modern Art, Carnegie Hall, Rockefeller Center, the Theater District, Central Park, St. Patrick's Cathedral, and myriad other attractions.

    Clement, the hotel's acclaimed restaurant, showcases Mediterranean cuisine. The Gotham Lounge provides an elegant setting for afternoon tea, light refreshments, and cocktails. Offering stunning views, Salon de Ning is a Manhattan hot spot. A cooked-to-order breakfast (surcharge) is also available. For more private dining, there's 24-hour room service.

    The health club (renovated September 2008) at the Peninsula New York features a cardio room with city views, a skylit weight room, and a fitness studio with complimentary group classes. The full-service, award-winning Peninsula Spa offers a wide range of treatments and massages. For families with little ones supervised childcare/activities is on offer.

    Complimentary wireless Internet access is available throughout the hotel.

    A complimentary shoe shine service is also available.

    Where to Eat

    Clement - Clement is an exciting new restaurant and bar concept that has elevated the dining experience at Peninsula New York to new heights. Clement brings patrons a fresh design and presents a seasonal Contemporary American menu utilizing locally-sourced ingredients from the northeast.

    Bar at Clement - The Bar at Clement provides a chic and seductive setting for cocktails and light, Asian-inspired plates overlooking Fifth Avenue.

    Salon de Ning - Glass-enclosed bar on top floor, with spectacular city skyline views from indoors and out on 2 furnished terraces. Seductive lighting, classic and creative cocktails, and an intimate interior bar. Open evenings, except Sunday.

    Gotham Lounge - Library-themed lounge with quiet, clublike ambience. Tall, arched windows overlooking the street. Open daily for lavish afternoon tea, cocktails, light fare, and desserts.

    Nearby Things to Do

    On the top level, a 42-foot glass-enclosed pool, paired with a spa tub, offers wonderful views of Midtown Manhattan. The serene pool boasts sleek dcor that features striking floor-to-ceiling teak columns set against a cool black tile floor. Poolside dining is also available, featuring a selection of Naturally Peninsula dishes that are both healthy and delicious. Water-aerobics classes are complimentary.

    The Peninsula New York's tri-level spa and health club (renovated September 2008) is considered one of the best in Manhattan. The health club features a cardio room with about 25 machines, along with plasma TVs and city views. Additional offerings include a skylit weight room and an exercise studio with complimentary yoga, Pilates, jazz, body-sculpting, and other classes daily. Locker rooms have marble showers, saunas, steam rooms, and a selection of workout attire that guests may borrow. The pool and health club are open daily from early morning until evening.

    Central Park, four blocks north, encompasses 843 acres of meadows, woods, cliffs, ravines, gardens, paths, and lakes in an emerald setting. Popular walking and running routes include Central Park Drive (closed to traffic during scheduled hours) and the path around the Jacqueline Kennedy Onassis Reservoir. The Loeb Boathouse, on a picturesque lake, rents rowboats and bicycles. The Wollman Rink offers ice-skating in the winter, the Victorian Gardens Amusement Park during the warmer months, and splendid views of the park and city year-round.

    Spa Services

    Discover an urban oasis high above the city at the award-winning Peninsula Spa. A selection of signature treatments that blend Asian, European and Ayurvedic philosophies creates a unique holistic experience. Guests enjoy advanced therapies, inventive spa cuisine, a cutting edge fitness center, a glass-enclosed pool and a sun terrace with panoramic city views.

    The Peninsula Spa is an intimate, indulgent environment featuring innovative spa treatments, magnificent facilities and the most gracious, expert service. Leave the world behind and try one of the popular facials by the French luxury skincare line Biologique Recherche or opt for the ultimate in relaxation with the signature Deep Tissue Experience. The Spa features a stunning collection of 10 treatment rooms including a spacious private spa suite, nail care suite, mens and womens private relaxation lounges, an Asian tea lounge, and changing rooms with luxurious heat experiences.
